Marci's Favorite Movies of All Time

These are my top three, in no particular order.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

This is one I just keep coming back to. It's beautifully shot, cleverly written, and lightheartedly fun.

Call Me By Your Name

I know that for many people, the storyline leaves much to be desired. And I understand that. But I love this movie for its ambience. It really captures a feeling of summer that few other movies have or can.

Pride and Prejudice

I mean, need I say anything? It's a damn classic.
